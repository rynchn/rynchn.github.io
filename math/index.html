Ruiyuan (Ronnie) Chen

I am a CRM-Simons postdoc at McGill University, working primarily with Anush Tserunyan.

I received my Ph.D. in 2018 from Caltech, under the supervision of Alexander Kechris.

My research is in mathematical logic, centered around descriptive set theory and connections with dynamical systems and category theory.
